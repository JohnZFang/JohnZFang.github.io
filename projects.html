SYDE 632 | Optimization Methods
This project is extracted from my research paper, the optimization solver YALMIP and MOSEK are in this project, Just for supplement, in CO 602, I also self-learned CVX solver in MATLAB to find the optimal solution of an optimization problem successfully, the code as follows, (constants n,C,b are omitted.) 

cvx_begin
variable x(n);
minimize (pow_pos(quad_over_lin(x(1),x(2))+quad_over_lin(x(2),x(1)),2)+b*x+10+abs(x(3)+5))
subject to
square_pos(x'*x+1)+pow_pos(x(1),4)+pow_pos(x(2),4)+pow_pos(x(3),4)<=100;
max([(C*x(1:2))'*(C*x(1:2)) x(1) x(2)])<=40;%square_pos(norm(C*x(1:2),2))<=40;
x(1)>=1;
x(2)>=1;
cvx_end
